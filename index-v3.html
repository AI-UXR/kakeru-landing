FAQ

Common questions

Kakeru (駆ける — "to run, to draw") is a web-based route designer that lets you draw shapes, text, or freehand art on a map. It snaps your drawing to real roads and trails, then exports it as a GPX file or creates the route directly on Strava — so you can run your art.
Kakeru Pro is $5/month, billed monthly. You can cancel anytime. The subscription gives you unlimited exports, freehand drawing, text-to-route, AI shape generation, and priority road data.
No. You can draw and preview routes without any account. A Strava connection is only needed to push routes directly to Strava — you can also just download the GPX file and import it to any GPS device.
Kakeru achieves 98% accuracy in road snapping using real road network data. Routes follow actual streets, trails, and paths. You can also toggle off-road mode for wilderness areas where road snapping doesn't apply.
Absolutely. Kakeru supports Running, Cycling, Hiking, and Off-Road activity modes. Each one optimizes road snapping for the right surfaces, path types, and trail networks.
Text-to-route lets you type any word or phrase (up to 15 characters) and kakeru converts the letterforms into a runnable GPS route. Perfect for running your name, a message, or a special date on the map.
